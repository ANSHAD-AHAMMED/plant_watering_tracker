🌱 About PlantCare

Your personal plant care assistant, designed to help you keep your green friends thriving with smart reminders and easy tracking.

Our Mission

We believe everyone deserves to have healthy, thriving plants in their life. PlantCare was created to bridge the gap between plant enthusiasts and successful plant parenting by providing simple, effective tools for plant care management.

Whether you're a seasoned green thumb or just starting your plant journey, PlantCare helps you build sustainable habits that keep your plants happy and healthy.
